Welcome to Himchuli Secondary School

About Us

Located in the historic city of Bhaktapur, Himchuli Secondary School has been a cornerstone of academic excellence and holistic education since its establishment. Our school is dedicated to fostering a nurturing and inclusive environment where every student can thrive and achieve their potential.

At Himchuli Secondary School, we focus on a balanced curriculum that integrates traditional values with modern educational practices. Our mission is to develop well-rounded individuals equipped with knowledge, skills, and values to face the challenges of the 21st century.

Our Vision

To empower students to excel academically, socially, and personally, creating a generation of responsible global citizens who contribute positively to society.

Our Facilities

  • State-of-the-art classrooms and laboratories
  • Comprehensive library with a wide range of resources
  • Extracurricular activities including sports, arts, and cultural programs
  • Supportive and qualified teaching staff
