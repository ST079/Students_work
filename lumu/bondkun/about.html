About us




Quality life through Quality education


Himchuli Academy, located in the historic city of Bhaktapur, Nepal, provides high-quality education to the youth of the region. Over the years, it has grown into a prominent educational institution, known for its dedication to both academic excellence and the holistic development of its students. The school offers a wide range of programs from early education to secondary levels, ensuring that each student receives the necessary skills and knowledge to thrive in a rapidly changing world.


In addition to its strong focus on core subjects, Himchuli Academy emphasizes extracurricular activities, including sports, arts, and cultural events, promoting creativity, teamwork, and leadership qualities among students. Our Mission

At Himchuli Academy, we are dedicated to providing a transformative educational experience that empowers students to achieve their full potential. Our mission is to cultivate a passion for learning, foster creativity, and prepare students to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Our History

Established in 2059 B.S, Himchuli Academy has been a cornerstone of academic excellence and community engagement. Over the decades, we have built a legacy of nurturing young minds, fostering innovation, and creating leaders of tomorrow.
