Welcome to
Himchuli Academy

Himchuli Academy, located in Dhuncho Pakha, Bhaktapur, Nepal, offers education up to grade 10. The school focuses on both academics and extracurricular activities to ensure the overall development of students. In addition to regular classes, students engage in sports, cultural programs, and various clubs, fostering creativity, leadership, and teamwork. The academy aims to provide a well-rounded education, preparing students for success both in and outside the classroom.

Our School


Himchuli Academy is a well-established educational institution. Our school is committed to providing quality education and emphasizes extracurricular activities to foster the overall development of its students.
